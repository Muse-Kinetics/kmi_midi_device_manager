Your firmware is either out of date, does not match this editor version, or a manual update was selected from the editor hardware menu.


It is highly recommended that you close all other programs and connect your device directly to your computer's USB port (no hub).


DO NOT disconnect your device during the update!


Clicking OK will begin the update process.


Clicking Cancel will abort the process. This editor will not be able to communicate with your device until you update the firmware.
